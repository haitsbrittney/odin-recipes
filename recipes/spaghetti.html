How to Make Spaghetti

What is Spaghetti?

Spaghetti is an Itlian dish consisting largely of pasta made in long, slender, solid strings--typically with sauce.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti noodles
  • Water
  • Spaghetti Sauce

Steps

  1. Boil Water
  2. Add Spaghetti noodles after water is boiled
  3. Drain Spaghetti noodles
  4. Add Spaghetti sauce onto the noodles
  5. Serve
